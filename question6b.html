Chicken Curry Ingredient

  • 800g Chicken
  • 1/2-1 Coconut Milk
  • 180g Curry Paste
  • 3 Large Potato
  • 3 Large Onion
  • 3 Stalks Curry Leaves

Chicken Curry Cooking Steps

  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant
  2. Add in chicken
  3. Continue to saute until chicken releases oil
  4. Add in Water, Tarmarind paste, Curry leaves, Potato, Onion, Sugar and Salt
  5. Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly
  6. Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate.
  7. Remove and Serve
